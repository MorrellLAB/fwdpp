Detailed Description

Simple model of crossing-over. Generates a Poisson-distributed number of recombination breakpoints with mean littler that are uniformly-distributed between minpos and maxpos.

Examples:
bneck_selection_ind.cc, diploid_binaryIO_ind.cc, diploid_ind.cc, diploid_ind_2locus.cc, and migsel_ind.cc.
